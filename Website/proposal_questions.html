What is the purpose of this study?

This study aims to gather data and present it in such a way that it can evaluate the effects of cryptocurrencies when compared to traditional currencies, and inform people of the crucial differences, especially relating to climate change and carbon dioxide emissions.

The target audience for this study is people within the ages of 15-50 that are interested in technology and/or the environment.

Our source of primary data is a google form that can be filled out here.

Our sources of secondary data will be information extrapolated from information gathered from estimations by trustworthy sources.

Our survey will be gathered anonymously from a wide range of people to avoid bias.

We will collect data with permission/consent and store it securely. We will make sure we use reputable data collection methods. De-identification is not necessary as the data will not contain any personal information or email addresses, etc. Any data acquired after a certain period of time will be destroyed. Data collected will only be accessible to the people required.
